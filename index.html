About Me

I am a full-stack software developer, interested in procedurally generated games, automated art creation, and electronically-assisted music.

The most important feature of a website, in my opinion, is that it is useable right away: long downloads, annoying sign-up processes, and tiresome pop-up advertisements kill the user experience.

My focus is to integrate these elements when necessary as seamlessly as possible, providing the site owner with their requirements while leaving the user experience intact.
